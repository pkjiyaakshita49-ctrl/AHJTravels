Explore Paris - The City of Lights

Trip Overview

Experience the beauty of Paris with this 5-day travel plan. Visit iconic landmarks, enjoy French cuisine, and explore the rich culture of one of the world's most romantic cities.

Travel Plan (5 Days)

Day 1

Arrival, hotel check-in, evening Seine River cruise.

Day 2

Visit Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Champs-Élysées.

Day 3

Explore Notre-Dame, Latin Quarter, and Montmartre.

Day 4

Day trip to Versailles Palace and gardens.

Day 5

Shopping, café visit, and departure.

Must Visit Places

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • Montmartre
  • Palace of Versailles

Travel Details

  • Best Time to Visit: April to June, September to October
  • Currency: Euro (€)
  • Language: French
  • Transport: Metro, buses, taxis
  • Visa: Schengen Visa required

  • Hotel (5 days): 430 $ - 1,000 $
  • Food & Activities: 210 $ - 430 $
