Explore London - The Royal Capital

Trip Overview

Experience London with this 5-day itinerary filled with royal landmarks, rich history, iconic attractions, and vibrant city culture. Discover the heart of the United Kingdom.

Day 1

Arrival, hotel check-in, and Thames riverside walk.

Day 2

Buckingham Palace, Big Ben, Westminster Abbey.

Day 3

Tower of London, Tower Bridge, London Eye.

Day 4

British Museum, Oxford Street shopping.

Day 5

Hyde Park visit and departure.
