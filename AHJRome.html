Discover Rome - The Eternal City

Trip Overview

Explore Rome with this 5-day itinerary filled with ancient history, stunning architecture, and authentic Italian cuisine. Discover the charm of one of the world's most historic cities.

Travel Plan (5 Days)

Day 1

Arrival, hotel check-in, evening walk around Piazza Navona.

Day 2

Visit Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Palatine Hill.

Day 3

Explore Vatican City, St. Peter's Basilica, and Sistine Chapel.

Day 4

Visit Trevi Fountain, Spanish Steps, and Pantheon.

Day 5

Shopping, local markets, and departure.

Must Visit Places

  • Colosseum
  • Roman Forum
  • Vatican City
  • Trevi Fountain
  • Pantheon

Travel Details

  • Best Time to Visit: April to June, September to October
  • Currency: Euro (€)
  • Language: Italian
  • Transport: Metro, buses, taxis
  • Visa: Schengen Visa required

Estimated Cost

  • Flight: 550 $ - 800 $
  • Hotel (5 days): 450 $ - 1,000 $
  • Food & Activities: 250 $ - 500 $
  • Total: 1,200 $ - 2,500 $
